Steve Jobs

American entrepreneur and co-founder of Apple Inc.

Here's a time line of steve jobs life:

  • 1955 - born in San Francisco, California

  • 1959 - the family had moved to the Monta Loma neighborhood in Mountain View, California

  • 1972- Jobs enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon.

  • 1973 - Steve Wozniak designed his own version of the classic video game Pong and gave the board to Jobs.

  • 1974 - Jobs returned to his parents' home in Los Altos and began looking for a job.

  • 1994 - interview, recalled that it took six months for him and Wozniak to figure out how to build the blue boxes.

  • 1975 - Jobs and Wozniak attended meetings of the Homebrew Computer Club

  • 1976 - Wozniak completed the basic design of the Apple

  • 1977 - Jobs and Wozniak introduced the Apple II at the West Coast Computer Faire

  • 1978 - Jobs was worth over $1 million
